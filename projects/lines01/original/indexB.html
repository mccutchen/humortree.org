Lines™
Lines™ is a randomly-generated background. It is 1 pixel wide and 1,200 pixels tall. There are 100 possible colors in Lines™.

It is generated by PHP. There are ten sections, each containing ten colors that are chosen randomly from 16,777,216 possible colors.

The colors are ordered in each section randomly.
Brent Gustafson, the creator of Vitaflo, should get a lot of credit for Lines™.

I first had the idea for randomly-generated background images that would be composed entirely of lines after seeing Vitaflo.

I think Vitaflo is a thing of beauty.
