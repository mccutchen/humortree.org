Press "Ctrl - R" for new images.

These images are all randomly generated, as the page loads. They are created by a process that (right now) involves 500 passes made over the image, with each pass "painting" either a line, a filled rectangle, a dotted line, or a dot on the image. The position, color, and size of each element is random. The specific colors available to the image are also random, as are the number of colors available.

This has been something i have been planning, hoping, wishing to do for about two years now.

* note: for some reason, the server sometimes returns duplicate images. There are even a few images that it returns repeatedly... so much that i have begun to recognize them immediately, and sort of think of them as friends...
