This site began as a sort of portfolio, slanted more towards being artistic (weird) than anything else. It was developed for Internet Explorer only (and the bulk of the older pages still work only in IE... I'm working to make them fully functional in any 5.0 browser). After a while, I started to make pages that were more technical than artistic. I still try to make each page aesthetically pleasing.
The projects are divided into two sections below- 1999/2000 and 2001. The 1999/2000 projects are meant to be viewed linearly, starting with project 1. The 2001 projects are the result of my shift in focus, and stand by themselves as experiments.

HTanimate 2.0
Javascript library that makes it simple to animate an element from one point to another in a given number of steps. Very easy to use. 		HTmove 2.0
